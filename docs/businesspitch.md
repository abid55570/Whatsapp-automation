# Business Pitch — cold outreach, unknown → trial

Goal: get stranger shopkeeper to try free trial in <2 min conversation.

## Hook lines (use first — 8 sec attention)

### Hindi (kirana / chai / restaurant — Tier 2/3)
> "Bhaisahab, WhatsApp pe customer 'menu bhejo' / 'open ho?' poochhte hain na? Maine ek tool banaya hai jo apne aap reply karta hai 24 ghante — ₹399 mahina, 14 din free. Dikha doon?"

### Hinglish (urban, English-comfortable)
> "Bhaiya aapka WhatsApp number customers pe diya hua hai? Hum ek bot banaya hai jo auto-reply karta hai — 'kya open hai', 'price', 'order' sab. ₹399 monthly, 14 days try free, koi card nahi maangte."

### English (salon/clinic/coaching)
> "Quick question — do customers WhatsApp you and you miss replies after-hours? I built a tool that auto-replies in their language — 14 days free, no card needed. Want a 60-sec demo?"

### Devanagari (printed flyer)
> "📱 आपकी दुकान का WhatsApp अब अपने आप जवाब देगा
> ✅ ₹399 महीना, 14 दिन फ्री
> ✅ Hindi/English दोनों में
> ✅ Order लेगा, payment link भेजेगा
> Demo: wa.me/919XXXXXXXXX"

## After hook — qualify in 30 sec

Ask these 3 (don't lecture):
1. **"Kitne message daily aate hain?"** → if 20+ → strong fit
2. **"Khud sambhalte ho ya koi staff?"** → "khud" = perfect ICP
3. **"Customer poochhta hai 'open ho?' 'menu?' 'price?' — kitni baar?"** → confirms pain

## Demo (60 sec, on YOUR phone)

Open your demo bot:
1. Show your phone WhatsApp → send "open ho?" to your demo bot
2. 2-sec reply in Hindi appears
3. Send "do atta 1 dal" → cart auto-built → confirm flow
4. Show admin dashboard on phone — "yeh aap dekh sakte ho khud, ya Sheet me prices badal sakte ho"

**Total time: 60 sec. Eye-contact. Don't read script.**

## Close (15 sec)

> "Toh fir kya kahte ho bhaiya? Mere paas QR hai, abhi 30 second me sign up — phone number daalo, WhatsApp pe code aayega, ho gaya. 14 din free, koi card nahi. Pasand aaya to ₹399, na aaya to bhool jaao, koi recurring nahi."

Hand them YOUR phone with signup page open. **Don't let them say "main baad mein karunga"** — that = lost.

## Trial → paid conversion (day 7 follow-up WA message)

> "Bhaiya namaste 🙏 Maine ek week pehle WhatsApp Auto laga ke gaya tha. Kuch aisa hua? Koi dikkat to nahi? Trial 7 din aur baki hai — ₹399 wala plan select karna ho to bata dena."

If they say "accha hai" → "Toh chaliye plan le lete hain abhi, future me 50% off lock ho jaata hai." (urgency)

If silent → day 12: "Bhaiya, 2 din me trial khatam ho jayega. ek baar dekh lo, agar nahi chahiye to koi baat nahi, automatically band ho jayega."

## Channels for cold reach

### Free / cheap
1. **Foot soldier** — walk 20 shops in your locality. 30% conversion typical.
2. **WhatsApp groups** — kirana sangh, restaurant association, salon owner groups
3. **Reels** — 30s in Hindi: shop owner before/after using bot. Hashtag #dukandar #kirana #smallbusinessindia
4. **District newspaper classified** — ₹300/ad in Dainik Jagran district edition

### Paid (when proven)
1. **FB/Insta ads** — geo-target "Lucknow + small business + 30-50 male"
2. **Razorpay merchant directory** — partner, listed in their tool marketplace
3. **Local kirana FMCG distributor** — distributor recommends to all dukandars, takes ₹50/conversion

## Cold WhatsApp script for stranger numbers (use sparingly — DLT compliance)

Don't spam. Personal intro only.

> "Namaste! Mera naam [you], main local hu, [city] mein. Maine ek tool banaya hai chhoti dukaano ke liye — WhatsApp pe customer ke msg ka auto reply, Hindi mein. Maine [common acquaintance] se aapka naam suna. Agar interest ho 60 second mein dikha doon? Reply karo 'haan' ya 'nahi'."

## What NOT to say
- ❌ "AI-powered" / "ChatGPT" — most kirana wallahs distrust AI. Say "auto-reply" or "bot".
- ❌ "Latest technology" — say "purana proven system, simple".
- ❌ "Free forever" — sounds suspicious. Say "14 din free trial, fir ₹399".
- ❌ Long demo — keep it 60 sec. Lose attention = lose sale.
- ❌ Email signup — use phone always.
