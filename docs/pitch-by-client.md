## 1. Kirana / General Store (most common ICP)

### Context
- 50–200 WhatsApp queries/day: "open hai?", "atta hai?", "kitne ka?", "deliver karoge?"
- Owner replies manually between serving in-store customers
- Loses 30%+ orders after 9 PM because owner stops replying

### Cold pitch (Hindi/Hinglish)
> "Bhaisahab namaste 🙏 Ek baat poochhna tha — aap WhatsApp pe customer ke msg dekhte ho na? 'Atta hai?' 'kitne ka?' 'open ho?' — yeh repeat msg din mein kitne baar aate hain?
>
> Maine ek bot banaya hai jo aapki dukaan ka WhatsApp 24 ghante apne aap reply karta hai. Customer 'do atta' likhega — bot order le lega, pickup time bhi pakka kar dega. Aapko sirf taiyaar karna hai.
>
> ₹399 mahina, 14 din free, no card. Ek minute me dikha doon kaise kaam karta hai?"

### Live demo (60 sec)
1. Show your phone → send "open ho?" to demo bot → instant Hindi reply
2. Send "do atta, ek dal" → cart appears with prices from sample sheet
3. "haan" → "5 baje pickup time pakka, cash on pickup confirmed"
4. "yeh sab aap dekh sakte ho — kal subah inbox kholo, raat ke 20 orders mil chuke honge"

### Top objections + answers
| Objection | Answer |
|---|---|
| "Mera Hindi me bot Hindi me jawab dega?" | "Haan. 6 bhashayein samajhta hai — Hindi, English, Hinglish, Bengali, Urdu, Bhojpuri. Customer jis mein likhega, uss mein jawab milega." |
| "Customer ko bot ka pata chal jaayega" | "Pata chalega bhi to kya — uska kaam to ho gaya. Aap chaho to manual mode rakho, sirf inbox dekho." |
| "Mera staff hi reply kar leta hai" | "Sunday ko? Raat ko? Holiday ko? Tab bot kaam aata hai." |
| "₹399 zyada hai" | "Ek atta ka pack ₹500 ka hai. Ek bhi order recover kara to bot ne paisa wasool kar diya." |

### Setup steps on cx phone
1. Scan QR → signup (3 min)
2. Connect WhatsApp Business via Meta Embedded Signup (2 min)
3. Pre-fill 3 things: hours, location, "do you deliver?" reply (1 min)
4. Connect Google Sheet with their prices (optional, 2 min)
5. Done. Total 8 min.

### Upgrade trigger
- Month 2: "Aap monthly ₹50k+ business kar rahe ho. Tax Pack ₹299 leke saari GST filing aaram se ho jayegi."
- Month 4: "Itne sale ho rahe hain, AI add-on ₹699 le lo — alag-alag questions ka jawab khud sambhal lega."

## 4. Clinic / Doctor / Dentist

### Context
- Appointment requests, prescription queries, follow-ups
- Receptionist costs ₹10k–₹15k/mo
- HIPAA-equivalent privacy concerns (be careful with messaging)
- Often closed for emergencies — patients can't reach

### Cold pitch
> "Doctor sahab, namaste. Aapke clinic ka WhatsApp number patients ko diya hua hai na? 'Aaj available ho?' 'Slot khali hai?' 'Report ready hai?' — yeh repeat queries aate hain.
>
> Mera tool yeh auto handle karta hai — timings, address, appointment booking sab. Emergency cases automatically flag karta hai. ₹399 mahina."

### Demo flow
1. Send "clinic kab open hai" → hours + location
2. Send "appointment kal" → booking link
3. Send "emergency hai chest pain" → bot says "DM the doctor immediately" + flags in owner inbox as high-priority red

### Privacy emphasis
> "Patient ka data India ke server pe, encrypted. HIPAA nahi par DPDP 2023 compliant hai. Bot kabhi medical advice nahi deta — sirf appointment + info."

### Objections
| Objection | Answer |
|---|---|
| "Receptionist khud kar leti hai" | "Sham 7 baje ke baad? Sunday? Holiday? Tab bot kaam aata hai. Aur receptionist ko break dega." |
| "Patient privacy ka kya?" | "Sirf aapke dashboard pe data dikhega. Encrypted at rest. Aap chaho to apne aap delete kar do." |
| "Glich ho gaya to galat info gaya" | "Aap pre-fill karte ho — galat info hi nahi ho sakti. Aur AI off rakho — sirf pre-set replies use ho." |

### Upgrade trigger
- Month 1: Tax Pack ₹299 (most clinics have GST + need monthly filing)
- Month 3: Growth ₹999 if 1500+ appointments/mo

## Universal pre-pitch checklist (carry to every visit)

- [ ] Your phone with demo bot active (test message before walking in)
- [ ] Printed QR code → links to signup with `?ref=field` tracking
- [ ] 2-min video on phone: "yeh bot kaise kaam karta hai" Hindi explainer
- [ ] Testimonial video from happy customer (after month 1)
- [ ] Business card with your direct WhatsApp
- [ ] Pen + notebook to capture phone numbers for follow-up
- [ ] Power bank (you'll demo a lot)

## Universal post-pitch follow-up

**Day 0 (right after visit):**
"Bhaiya namaste 🙏 Aaj milne ka shukriya. Setup link: https://yourdomain.com/signup?ref=fieldname. Trial 14 din free, koi card nahi."

**Day 7:**
"Kuch dikkat ho rahi hai bot mein? Setup ke liye kabhi bhi WhatsApp karo."

**Day 12:**
"2 din mein trial khatam ho jayega. ₹399 me continue karna ho to abhi plan select karo — auto-debit, kabhi bhi cancel."

**Day 18 (if didn't pay):**
"Bot band ho gaya hai? Phir se chalu karna chahte ho? Plan select karo, 1 minute me wapas chalu ho jayega."

## Conversion math (use this to track yourself)

| Stage | Drop-off | Action if too low |
|---|---|---|
| Cold conversation → demo accepted | 50% (worst) | Improve hook line |
| Demo → signup | 60% | Improve demo (60 sec strict!) |
| Signup → WA connect | 65% | Walk them through Embedded Signup |
| Trial → paying | 25% (worst) | Better follow-up cadence |

If your funnel is worse than this — fix the lowest-conversion stage first.

## Final words

Every script above is a starting point. **Talk like a friend, not a salesman.** Eye contact. Sit at their level (not standing while they sit). Drink their chai if offered. Tell them your name. Ask theirs.

**Sale closes on trust, not features.**
