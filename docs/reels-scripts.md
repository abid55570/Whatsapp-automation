# Reels / Shorts Scripts — by client type

For Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, WhatsApp Status. 30-60 second format.

Each reel structure:
- **0-3s** Hook (most critical — earn the watch)
- **3-8s** Problem (relatable pain)
- **8-25s** Demo / Solution (visual)
- **25-30s** CTA (clear next step)

Filming tips at the end.

## Filming tips (zero-budget)

### Equipment (₹0)
- Your phone (rear camera, 1080p)
- Daylight only — no expensive lights
- Phone tripod ₹400 (one-time)
- Headphone mic with phone ₹200 (one-time)

### Filming rules
- **Vertical only** (9:16) — Reels native
- Hold phone steady; use a wall or tripod
- 60 fps if available — looks more pro
- Outside light: shoot 9-11 AM or 3-5 PM (golden hour)
- Inside: face a window, never have window behind you
- Record AUDIO separately if shop is noisy — use voice memo, sync later

### Editing (free)
- **CapCut** (free app) — has Hindi templates
- **InShot** — second choice
- Add captions ALWAYS (80% watch without sound)
- Use trending audio (CapCut suggests)
- Keep cuts every 2-3 sec for retention

### Hindi/Hinglish text style
- Use **bold sans-serif** fonts (CapCut "Poppins" or "Inter")
- White text with black outline (readable on any background)
- Maximum 6-7 words per screen
- Translate emoji to "अप्रैल" → "April" (use Roman script for clarity)

### Posting schedule
- **Monday/Wednesday/Friday** 8 AM (morning rush)
- **Tuesday/Thursday/Saturday** 7 PM (evening peak engagement)
- WhatsApp Status: post EVERY reel as a story
- Cross-post to Facebook + LinkedIn (less competition for local biz audience)

### Hashtag strategy
- 5 micro tags (under 100k posts): #lucknowbusiness #indorebusiness #yourcity
- 5 medium tags (100k-1M): #smallbusinessindia #kirana #dukandar
- 3 big tags (1M+): #india #business #entrepreneur

### Track what works
| Metric | Target | If below |
|---|---|---|
| 3-sec view rate | 70%+ | Hook is bad — change first 3 sec |
| 50% completion | 40%+ | Slow demo — speed up |
| Saves | 1-2% | Add useful info in demo |
| Comments | 5+/100 views | Ask a question in caption |
| DMs | 1/200 views | Stronger CTA in last 5 sec |

## Final word

**Don't sound like a salesman.** Sound like a friend who solved a problem and is excited to share. The bot is the hero, you're just the helpful neighbor.

Your face IS your branding. Show your face in at least 1 frame per reel — builds trust massively.

Quality < consistency. **Post daily for 30 days, then evaluate.** Most viral reels come from week 4–6.
